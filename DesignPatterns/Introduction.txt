GOF design patterns are divided into three groups : 

1) Creational design patterns : These patterns will tell how objects and classes are created
Examples : 
Singleton, 
Prototype, 
Factory, 
Builder.

2) Structural design patterns : These patterns define how the structure of your classes should be.

Decorator,
Facade

3) Behavioural design patterns : These patterns defines how classes communicate with each other
Examples : 
Observer, 
Chain of responsibility, 
Strategy
